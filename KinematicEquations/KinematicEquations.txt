u (initial velocity)
v (final velocity)
a (acceleration)
t (time)

1) s = (u + v / 2) * t

2) v = u + a * t

3) s = u * t + (a*t^2 / 2)

4) s = v * t - (a*t^2 / 2)

5) v^2 = u^2 + 2 * a * s
